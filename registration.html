Application Process

To register for the event, please complete the Registration form. After submitting the form, successful candidates will receive official letters of acceptance on a rolling basis, serving as confirmation of their acceptance.

We would like to emphasize that registration is completely free of charge. Don't miss out on this opportunity to be part of the event.

Registration form

We offer two modes of participation: in-person attendance or remote participation (available exclusively for non-Moldovan applications). Limited travel fellowships are available for in-person attendees.
