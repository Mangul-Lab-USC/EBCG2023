Welcome

We are delighted to extend an invitation to students and interested individuals to join us for the second edition of the East European Bioinformatics and Computational Genomics School (EEBG). This year's event promises to be an exceptional in-person and virtual learning opportunity, running from June 26 to July 7, în Chișinău, Moldova. Designed specifically for students and researchers in the early stages of their careers in Eastern Europe, EEBG2023 School will provide a comprehensive exploration of bioinformatics and computational biology.

We have a fantastic lineup of speakers, including scientists from the top universities in the United States, Switzerland, Denmark, Turkey, Poland, and Romania, who will deliver a range of lectures, keynote presentations, and hands-on sessions. In addition, we are thrilled to announce that the Executive Editor of GigaScience journal will be joining us to give a keynote presentation. GigaScience is an open access, open data, open peer-review journal that focuses on 'big data' research from the life and biomedical sciences.

Participants can choose to attend in person or remotely, and we are pleased to offer a limited number of travel fellowships for those who choose to attend in person. If you are interested in applying for a travel fellowship or registering for the event, please visit our website at Registration

Don't miss this opportunity to engage with leading experts in bioinformatics and computational biology and to broaden your knowledge in these emerging fields.

We are excited to announce that upon completion of the program, a Certificate in Bioinformatics will be offered to individuals who attend all lectures and successfully complete an individual project. This certificate will serve as recognition of the skills and knowledge gained through participation in the East European Bioinformatics and Computational Genomics School (EEBG).

EEBG2023 is an initiative of the Bioinformatics Laboratory of Technical University of Moldova in partnership with „Nicolae Testemitanu” State University of Medicine and Pharmacy of the Republic of Moldova and Mangul Lab at the University of Southern California School of Pharmacy, supported by BioTech Association from Moldova, USAID, and Sweden.
